Pre-Class Reflection

#BeforeReflectionAndAlgorithm (Sun 6 pm, Mon 10 am) Template

  • 1. #ShareableMessage (1-3 messages/points): 
  • #InsightfulComponent: What are 1-3 insightful components from the readings? Can you express this as a message to share with someone else? For example, a linkedin, twitter, instagram post?
  • What #ScreenshotOrCopyPaste can you include from the reading?Why was this really cool and exciting to learn?
  • #NextStep: What is a #NextStep based on what you learned? If you'd never learned it, you'd have done A. But since you did, what might be B? For example, can you try to apply it to your everyday life? Can you talk about it with someone? What is a question you might ask next time you're in a specific situation, what is a question it might lead you to ask, an Reflect on how what you experienced The goal is #HowCouldMakeADifference- how could what you learned lead to you doing something differently, or at least help you see how you might have taken a different action in the past (if you'd known this), or in the future (moving forward).

    • #CommentOrDiscussion: What is a comment/question you'd raise for discussion in Discord #AndOr BreakoutRooms? What is a #PrototypeAnswer? [#BadButUseful!]
    • #SocialConnection: How will you engage with someone this week?
      • In the class, what will you do or say in Discord/BreakoutRoom?
      • Outside the class, what message will you send to a specific person or a group to set a time to #Cowork on readings, and/or to have an #AccountabilityBuddy to check in if you worked on it at the time you were planning to?

Post-Class Reflection

#AfterReflectionAlgorithm (Tue 6pm, Wed 10 am) Template

  • 1. #ShareableMessage (1-3 messages/points): 
  • #InsightfulComponent: What are 1-3 insightful components from the class that someone said. How did my thinking change after class?
  • #ContentToShare: What are 1-3 facts, ideas or concepts you learned from the class and why they might be useful?

[PUT ANSWER HERE]

  • 2. #Accountability:
    • What message did I send or action I did during class? Screenshot 1-3 things that you put into Discord, onto Figjam, or what you said.
    • How did I connect with someone in a class in a way that was #MutuallyBeneficial? E.g. share information, understand them better, ask for advice?
    • How many minutes were my video on? 
    • How else did I contribute to the course and my learning?
    • How did your plan from last week go? What worked well, what didn't? What are you going to try differently this week? Who are you going to ask for help? More advice at: #Planning
